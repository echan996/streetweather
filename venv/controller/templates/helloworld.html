The Great Discovery

The Garbanzo Bean was first discovered by the ancient Brombloms in the summer of 1482. King Mlem of the Bromblom Empire ordered Jonkolotong to hand him the bean. Jonkolotong refused, stating that he wanted to keep the bean for himself. He was executed.

Shortly after King Mlem took possession of the Garbanzo Bean, he placed it on his bedside table so that his bedroom wouldn't be so dark at night. King Mlem was very afraid of the dark. Within a few days, he noticed that he felt more full of energy than he ever did in his life. And within a few weeks, he started gaining immense physical strength.
